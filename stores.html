Where to find us

Stop by our Klitmøller Collective store, in the heart of Klitmøller.

We are very proud of our local Klitmøller Collective store on the mainroad of Klitmøller. You will experience the passion and the lifestyle of our most beloved brand. A big part of Klitmøller Collective is the history within this authentic fishing village, where surfers and fishermen unite as the town we know today. Hopefully you will get a taste of that, if visting us in our store.
