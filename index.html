Klitmøller Collective is a lifestyle. Our ideas originate from encounters, journeys and casual gatherings from a lifetime of generations, bound together by the sea in and around Klitmøller. We design for people who are drawn to the raw Nordic nature. Our aim is to design and produce things that last in quality materials with a sustainable approach to materials, production and look.
